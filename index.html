Research

My research interests include robotics, robot learning, computer vision, and machine learning. My personal goal in robotics research is to develop truly autonomous robots with multiple functions that can reason about and adapt to new environments or scenarios. I am particularly interested in leveraging deep-learning techniques to enhance robot capabilities. Currently, I am working on diffusion models to improve robot policies for single-agent and multi-agent autonomous exploration.
